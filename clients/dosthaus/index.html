Welcome to Dosthaus

The ultimate destination for Korean toast, coffee, and dessert lovers in India.

Our Menu Book a Table Zomato Swiggy

Now in Gurugram

At Dosthaus, we offer a variety of delicious and authentic Korean toast, coffee, and desserts that will make your taste buds happy. Whether you crave for a sweet and crunchy honey butter toast, a smooth and aromatic latte, or a refreshing Mocktail, we have it all for you.

  • Cozy and calming atmosphere, Friendly staff, relaxing and soothing music.
  • Wide range of drinks and desserts, from traditional to modern.
  • Fresh ingredients, sophisticated equipment, and creative recipes.

Our cafe is more than just a place to enjoy coffee and snacks.
It's also a space where people can socialize, relax, study, work, or express themselves
