The beginning

Our humble beginnings were in maintenance contracts, where we learned the ropes of the industry. We worked hard and earned a reputation for quality and reliability. Soon, we caught the eye of some korean clients, who were looking for builders to construct their luxury residential houses. It was a huge opportunity for us, a chance to showcase our skills and creativity.

  • 2010 - Established HANKUK construction and trading pvt. Ltd.
  • 2011 - Construction contract 6 items / Made first maintenance contract.
  • 2012 - Built new luxury house as residential house in Korea.

Rapid Growth of company

We had finally made it to Mumbai, the city of dreams. Our sales had skyrocketed in the past few Years, and we were ready to expand our business. We decided to launch a new venture, a company that specialized in acrylic and printing manufacturing.

  • 2013 - Rapid increment of company a years sales.
  • 2014 - Contract first interior and enter in Mumbai.
  • 2015 - Developed as professional acrylic and printing manufacturing company.

Reliability and distinction in construction

We had a big ambition to become the first vendor for Samsung c&t, one of the leading construction companies in the world.

We knew it was not easy, but we were ready to take the challenge. We brainstormed for new business ideas, looking for gaps in the market and opportunities to grow.

We also prepared our registration documents, making sure we met all the requirements and standards. We wanted to impress Samsung c&t with our vision and quality, and secure our place as their trusted partner.

  • 2017 - Take suggestion for new businesses and registration for Samsung c&t as 1st vendor
